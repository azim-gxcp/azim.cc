## Critical Framing Note

GX Protocol does not position itself as an enemy of the existing financial system. It positions itself as **what money looks like when designed for participants, not institutions**. The analysis in these documents exists to understand the landscape — not to attack it. Content derived from this research should follow the GX positioning: **name the structural problem, present the design alternative, let the audience draw their own conclusions**.

Never:
- Sound like a conspiracy channel
- Use accusatory language against specific individuals or institutions
- Claim GX will "overthrow" or "replace" anything
- Position GX as a protest movement

Always:
- Lead with verifiable data
- Present structural analysis, not blame
- Frame GX as a design question: "What if money were designed differently?"
- Invite scrutiny of both the current system and GX's alternative
