### Full Description
GXC Protocol Foundation is a non-profit organization headquartered in Zug, Switzerland, building GX Coin — a sovereign digital currency protocol for productive economics.

Unlike speculative cryptocurrencies, GX Coin is designed as economic infrastructure: a fixed-supply (1.25 trillion), gold-anchored digital currency distributed for free to individuals through a Genesis Distribution. The protocol embeds immutable monetary policies including 0% interest rates, demurrage-based circulation incentives, and capital provision through profit-sharing rather than debt.

The protocol runs on Proof of Authority consensus with professional validators and is governed by a three-body structure (Guardian Council, Technocratic Senate, Judicial Oracle) that transitions from guided oversight to full community governance over 25 years.

GX Coin is intentionally not listed on any exchange. Its value is determined by purchasing power within its own economy, not by speculative markets.

The project has been in development since October 2023, originally under the name "Goodness Exchange." The commercial operations arm, GXC Protocol PTE LTD, is registered in Singapore.
