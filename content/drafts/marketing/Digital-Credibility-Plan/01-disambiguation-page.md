# GX Coin Protocol - Who We Are (And Who We Are Not)

GX Coin Protocol is a sovereign digital currency protocol for productive economics, founded in October 2023 and managed by the **GXC Protocol Foundation** (non-profit, Zug, Switzerland), with commercial operations through **GXC Protocol PTE LTD** (Singapore).

We are aware that several other blockchain projects share similar names. To protect our participants and ensure clarity, we want to be explicit:

---

## We Are NOT Affiliated With:

### GXChain (GXC)
- **What it is:** A data exchange blockchain founded in China, traded on exchanges including Coinbase and Binance
- **Ticker:** GXC
- **Website:** gxchain.org
- **Relationship to us:** None. Different team, technology, codebase, mission, and legal entity.

### GXCOIN, Inc. (gxcoin.world)
- **What it is:** A privately held company incorporated in Hawaii, USA (founded 2005), offering Web3 banking and marketing products
- **Website:** gxcoin.world
- **Relationship to us:** None. Different company, jurisdiction, product, and leadership.

### Grindery X (GX)
- **What it is:** An ERC-20 utility token on Ethereum, listed on CoinGecko and other aggregators
- **Ticker:** GX
- **Relationship to us:** None. Different token, blockchain, team, and purpose.

## If You've Been Directed Here by a Third-Party Site

Some cryptocurrency aggregators (CoinMarketCap, CoinGecko, ICOHolder, Blockspot) list projects called "GX Coin" or "GXC" that are **not us**. If a listing shows exchange trading, price charts, or market cap data, it is referring to a different project.

GX Coin Protocol does not trade on any exchange and has no market price. This is a deliberate design principle, not a limitation.
