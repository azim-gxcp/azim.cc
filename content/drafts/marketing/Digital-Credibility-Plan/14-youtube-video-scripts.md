## VIDEO 1: "What is GX Coin Protocol?" (3 minutes)

### Visual Style
Clean motion graphics. Minimal. Think Kurzgesagt-lite. Icons, simple animations, text callouts. No faces.

### Script

**[0:00 - 0:20] HOOK**

[Visual: Globe spinning, with currency symbols ($, EUR, BTC) swirling around it, then dissolving]

NARRATOR: "Every day, billions of people work. They build, they create, they serve. And every day, the money they earn loses value. Not because their work got worse — but because the monetary system wasn't designed to preserve it. GX Coin Protocol is designed to change that."

**[0:20 - 0:50] THE PROBLEM**

[Visual: Split screen — left shows person working hard, right shows a shrinking money stack]

NARRATOR: "Traditional currencies lose purchasing power through inflation. Central banks print more money, and the money you already earned buys less. Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin tried to fix this by capping supply — but they created a different problem: hoarding. When money goes up in value just by holding it, nobody spends it. And a currency nobody spends isn't a currency — it's a speculative asset."

**[0:50 - 1:30] THE SOLUTION**

[Visual: GX Coin logo appears. Clean, stable.]

NARRATOR: "GX Coin Protocol takes a different approach. It's a sovereign digital currency with a fixed supply of 1.25 trillion units — anchored to gold at 1 GX per gram. The supply can never be expanded. But unlike Bitcoin, GX Coin is designed to circulate, not to be hoarded."

[Visual: Units flowing between people, businesses, governments — a circular animation]

NARRATOR: "How? Three mechanisms. First: zero percent interest. Capital is provided through profit-sharing, not debt. Second: demurrage — a small progressive fee on large idle balances that encourages money to keep moving. Third: free distribution. You don't buy GX units. You receive them through a Genesis Distribution — your founding stake in a new economy."

**[1:30 - 2:10] HOW IT'S DIFFERENT**

[Visual: Comparison table appearing item by item]

NARRATOR: "GX Coin is not a token. It's not listed on any exchange — by design. There's no ICO, no presale, no investors to pay back. The protocol is managed by a non-profit foundation registered in Zug, Switzerland. Core monetary policies — the fixed supply, the interest prohibition — are permanently embedded in the blockchain. No governance body can change them."

**[2:10 - 2:45] WHO IT'S FOR**

[Visual: Three icons — person, building, government building]

NARRATOR: "For individuals: a currency that preserves the value of your work. For businesses: interest-free capital and a stable operating environment. For governments: transparent monetary infrastructure without debt obligations."

**[2:45 - 3:00] CLOSE**

[Visual: GX Coin logo + website]

NARRATOR: "GX Coin Protocol. A productive economy for the future. Learn more at gxcoin.money."

A farmer's harvest doesn't change in value because a trader in another country decided to sell."
