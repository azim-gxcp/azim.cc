# Tier 1 — Main Cast (10 Recurring Characters)

These ten characters carry the GX story across every video series. They are the faces the audience recognizes, grows attached to, and follows through multiple scenarios. Every episode should feature at least one main cast member.

---

## 1. MAYA — Teacher (29)

- **Silhouette**: Medium height, average build, shoulder-length hair with a soft outward flip. Always carries a book or tablet.
- **Skin tone**: Wheat
- **Hair**: Warm dark brown, shoulder-length with a gentle outward curl at the ends. Side-parted.
- **Face**: Oval face, almond eyes, soft smile, small round glasses (thin gold frames).
- **Body**: Slim-average, relaxed posture.
- **Signature outfit**: Mustard-yellow cardigan over a soft cream blouse, olive-green long skirt, simple flats.
- **Signature colors**: Harvest Gold, Forest, Cream
- **Props**: Hardcover book, small cotton tote bag, chalkboard pointer, a worn notebook.
- **Personality**: Patient, curious, explains things in simple words. The "translator" of the cast — she makes complex ideas accessible.
- **Story role**: The explainer-protagonist. When a concept needs to be unpacked for the audience, Maya is often the one asking the question or giving the answer. She receives her monthly grant and uses it for school supplies and groceries — illustrating the everyday-use scenario.

---

## 2. ARUN — Farmer (32)

- **Silhouette**: Tall, broad shoulders, tan forearms, wide-brimmed straw hat when outdoors.
- **Skin tone**: Chestnut
- **Hair**: Black, short crop with a slight side sweep. Sometimes hidden under his hat.
- **Face**: Square jaw, kind steady eyes, faint smile lines, clean-shaven.
- **Body**: Sturdy, muscular but not exaggerated.
- **Signature outfit**: Moss-green rolled-up long-sleeve shirt, earth-brown trousers, canvas boots, a faded red bandana around the neck.
- **Signature colors**: Moss, Terracotta, Ochre
- **Props**: Wooden crate of vegetables, a basket of eggs, a rake or hoe, a phone in his shirt pocket for checking the GX wallet.
- **Personality**: Grounded, practical, slow-speaking, skeptical-but-open. The "show me it works" character.
- **Story role**: Primary producer. Represents the rural economy, food sovereignty, and the first merchant in a village to accept GX for his produce. Sends GX to his brother in the city. Receives grants. Asks: "if I grow food, will GX buy me seed?"

---

## 3. NADIA — Nurse (26)

- **Silhouette**: Petite, upright posture, hair pulled back, always a stethoscope visible.
- **Skin tone**: Almond
- **Hair**: Dark brown, pulled into a low bun with a few loose strands framing the face.
- **Face**: Round face, large warm eyes, gentle smile, a small freckle near the left eye.
- **Body**: Slim, poised.
- **Signature outfit**: Sky-blue scrub top with a white undershirt, matching scrub pants, white sneakers, a light cardigan over her shoulders when off-duty.
- **Signature colors**: Sky, Paper, Coral
- **Props**: Stethoscope, clipboard, water bottle, a phone with the GX wallet open.
- **Personality**: Empathetic, steady under pressure, soft-spoken but firm. The "essential worker" voice.
- **Story role**: Essential services. Shows how care workers receive grants, save in GX, and send some to her mother in another country. Explains velocity through everyday small transactions.

---

## 4. KAI — Carpenter (28)

- **Silhouette**: Athletic, medium-tall, tool belt, sleeves rolled up, visible forearms.
- **Skin tone**: Peach
- **Hair**: Sandy blond, short textured crop, slightly tousled.
- **Face**: Angular features, blue-gray eyes, stubble, a relaxed half-smile.
- **Body**: Athletic-average, capable posture.
- **Signature outfit**: Terracotta long-sleeve henley under a denim apron/work vest, full-length work trousers, brown work boots, a pencil behind the ear.
- **Signature colors**: Terracotta, Charcoal, Ochre
- **Props**: Hammer, hand saw, a carved wooden chair (his work), measuring tape clipped to the belt.
- **Personality**: Hands-on, makes things for a living, values fair pay, learns by doing.
- **Story role**: Skilled trades. His workshop becomes one of the first to price custom furniture in GX. Shows the "bridge" moment — when a trade starts denominating in GX alongside fiat. Borrows from the Business Loan Pool to buy a new lathe.

---

## 5. ZARA — Grocer / Shop Owner (30)

- **Silhouette**: Medium height, apron over clothing, standing behind a counter or arranging produce.
- **Skin tone**: Almond
- **Hair**: Black, mid-length wavy, often tied in a loose ponytail with a scarf-tie.
- **Face**: Oval, warm brown eyes, frequent smile, a small beauty mark on the cheek.
- **Body**: Average build, active posture.
- **Signature outfit**: Plum long-sleeve top under a tan canvas apron with front pockets, olive trousers, simple slip-on shoes.
- **Signature colors**: Plum, Ochre, Cream
- **Props**: Scale, notebook/ledger, QR code card at the counter, baskets of fruit and vegetables, a small cash drawer.
- **Personality**: Business-minded, warm with customers, keeps tight books, cautious but entrepreneurial.
- **Story role**: Local commerce. The **first merchant to accept GX** in her neighborhood. Her story illustrates the chicken-and-egg moment — a single shop tipping into GX-priced goods, and the effect on foot traffic, neighbors, and suppliers.

## 6. THEO — Software Engineer (27)

- **Silhouette**: Tall-medium, lean, laptop bag over shoulder, headphones around neck.
- **Skin tone**: Wheat
- **Hair**: Dark brown, medium length, swept back with a slight wave.
- **Face**: Long oval, sharp cheekbones, green eyes behind clear-frame glasses, stubble beard.
- **Body**: Lean, relaxed.
- **Signature outfit**: Teal hoodie over a graphic tee, dark jeans, minimalist white sneakers, a canvas messenger bag.
- **Signature colors**: Teal, Indigo, Charcoal
- **Props**: Laptop, phone, a small mechanical keyboard, a coffee cup, a GX wallet app in the foreground of many scenes.
- **Personality**: Curious about systems, verifies things himself, early adopter, explains tech to non-tech friends.
- **Story role**: The digital-economy participant. Gets paid in GX for freelance work. Shows cross-border GX transfers. Explains the protocol's technical integrity to skeptical characters without jargon.

## 7. LINA — University Student (19)

- **Silhouette**: Short-medium, backpack always on, phone in hand, earbuds in one ear.
- **Skin tone**: Peach
- **Hair**: Strawberry-blonde, long with a high ponytail, soft bangs.
- **Face**: Round, bright eyes, rosy cheeks, big expressive smile.
- **Body**: Slim, youthful energy.
- **Signature outfit**: Coral oversized sweater, navy-blue full-length jeans, cream-white sneakers, a yellow scrunchie on the wrist.
- **Signature colors**: Coral, Indigo, Cream
- **Props**: Backpack, laptop in a sleeve, water bottle with stickers, a lanyard.
- **Personality**: Eager, a quick learner, asks "why" a lot, spends her grant thoughtfully. The audience-proxy character for young viewers.
- **Story role**: First-time grant recipient at age 18. Her story introduces new viewers to the onboarding flow, KYC, and the first experience of purchasing power. She is often paired with Maya (who explains) and Kofi (younger, still in school).

## 8. SAM — Delivery Rider / Gig Worker (22)

- **Silhouette**: Medium height, backpack cube on the shoulders, helmet in hand, always mid-stride or on a scooter.
- **Skin tone**: Chestnut
- **Hair**: Black, tight curls cropped short on the sides with more volume on top.
- **Face**: Round-square, bright eyes, easy grin, a small gap between front teeth.
- **Body**: Athletic, active.
- **Signature outfit**: Bright yellow delivery jacket, black full-length cargo pants, red sneakers, full work gloves.
- **Signature colors**: Harvest Gold, Coral, Charcoal
- **Props**: Delivery cube backpack, scooter/bicycle, helmet, phone on a handlebar mount.
- **Personality**: Hustler in the best sense, multi-tasker, lives in the gig economy, tracks his daily earnings on the GX wallet.
- **Story role**: Gig worker receiving multiple small GX payments daily from partner merchants. Illustrates velocity — frequent, small, circulating transactions. Also shows how grants supplement gig income during slow days.

## 9. PRIYA — Accountant / Bookkeeper (31)

- **Silhouette**: Medium height, upright posture, a neat folder or laptop always in hand.
- **Skin tone**: Almond
- **Hair**: Black, sleek bob cut at the jawline, center-parted.
- **Face**: Oval, dark thoughtful eyes, thin rectangular glasses, calm smile.
- **Body**: Slim-average, composed.
- **Signature outfit**: Navy-indigo blazer over a cream button-up, cream trousers, simple dark flats, a slim watch.
- **Signature colors**: Indigo, Cream, Ochre
- **Props**: Laptop, ledger, calculator, a stack of receipts neatly clipped, a coffee mug.
- **Personality**: Precise, patient, good at translating numbers into plain speech. The "numbers translator" of the cast.
- **Story role**: Helps small businesses track GX alongside fiat in their books. Explains the velocity tax with actual numbers. When Zara's shop starts accepting GX, Priya helps her record it correctly. Bridges financial literacy for other characters.

## 10. JORDAN — Café Owner / Entrepreneur (29)

- **Silhouette**: Medium-tall, warm presence, apron over casual clothing, always moving around the café.
- **Skin tone**: Wheat
- **Hair**: Auburn-brown, medium-length wavy, often tied in a low half-bun.
- **Face**: Round-oval, warm hazel eyes, freckles across the nose, a bright open smile.
- **Body**: Average build, friendly posture.
- **Signature outfit**: Forest-green t-shirt under a cream linen apron with leather straps, dark jeans, brown boots, a small pendant necklace.
- **Signature colors**: Forest, Cream, Terracotta
- **Props**: Coffee cup, espresso machine in the background, a handwritten menu board, a tablet for taking GX payments.
- **Personality**: Entrepreneurial, community-minded, balances ideals with running a small business, open to new ideas but cautious with money.
- **Story role**: Small business owner who becomes an **early GX partner**. Applies to the Business Loan Pool to expand. Their café becomes a visible community hub where multiple characters cross paths — a natural setting for many story scenes.
