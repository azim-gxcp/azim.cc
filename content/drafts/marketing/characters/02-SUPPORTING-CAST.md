# Tier 2 — Supporting Cast (24 Scenario Characters)

These characters appear when a story needs their specific role. They are canonical — once illustrated, they never change — but they don't need to appear in every episode. Organized by thematic cluster.

---

## A. Primary Production & Trades (4)

### 11. FINN — Fisherman (33)

- **Silhouette**: Tall, broad, weathered, often with a net or bucket, rolled-up sleeves.
- **Skin tone**: Chestnut
- **Hair**: Dark brown, slightly wavy, medium length, sun-bleached tips, often held back by a rolled bandana.
- **Face**: Square jaw, soft laugh lines, sun-kissed cheeks, stubble.
- **Body**: Sturdy, strong forearms.
- **Signature outfit**: Teal long-sleeve henley, navy rubber overalls worn full with straps over both shoulders, rubber boots, a faded denim cap.
- **Signature colors**: Teal, Indigo, Ochre
- **Props**: Fishing net, crate of fish, small boat in the background, a GX wallet on a waterproof phone.
- **Personality**: Patient, weather-wise, knows his customers by name.
- **Story role**: Coastal economy. Sells his catch directly to Zara's shop and Jordan's café — illustrates short supply chains and GX-denominated trade between producers and retailers.

### 12. IVY — Artisan / Weaver (27)

- **Silhouette**: Petite, seated at a loom or craft bench, hands always doing something.
- **Skin tone**: Peach
- **Hair**: Auburn, long with a loose side braid over one shoulder, flower or small clip in the hair.
- **Face**: Heart-shaped, wide green eyes, gentle smile, a small scatter of freckles.
- **Body**: Slim, artistic posture.
- **Signature outfit**: Plum long dress or tunic with hand-stitched patterns, moss-green apron with pockets of thread, sandals.
- **Signature colors**: Plum, Moss, Ochre
- **Props**: Handmade textiles, a loom, spools of thread, a small wooden price tag.
- **Personality**: Creative, detail-loving, undervalues her own work until she learns to price it fairly in GX.
- **Story role**: Craft economy. Illustrates how artisans can reach global buyers directly via GX, bypassing middlemen. Also shows Business Loan Pool use for small-scale production expansion.

### 13. RAVI — Mechanic (30)

- **Silhouette**: Medium height, strong shoulders, grease-stained hands, wrench always within reach.
- **Skin tone**: Wheat
- **Hair**: Black, thick, swept back with a bit of length at the top, short on the sides.
- **Face**: Square, dark mustache trimmed neatly, confident grin.
- **Body**: Muscular-average, capable.
- **Signature outfit**: Charcoal-gray work coveralls worn fully and zipped up, long-sleeve white undershirt visible at collar and cuffs, work boots, a rolled rag in the back pocket.
- **Signature colors**: Charcoal, Ochre, Coral
- **Props**: Wrench, tire, a motorcycle or small car in the background, tool cart.
- **Personality**: Straight-talking, fair prices, loyal to regulars, trusts what he can take apart.
- **Story role**: Auto-repair trade. Shows how skilled trades transition to accepting GX. His shop takes GX for repairs; he uses GX to pay Ivy for the workshop curtains she wove.

### 14. ELSA — Baker (25)

- **Silhouette**: Medium height, flour-dusted apron, usually holding a tray or loaf.
- **Skin tone**: Peach
- **Hair**: Strawberry-blonde, tied up in a high bun with a dusting of flour, a few loose strands.
- **Body**: Average, warm posture.
- **Face**: Round, rosy cheeks, light blue eyes, small nose, constant cheerful smile.
- **Signature outfit**: White t-shirt under a rose-colored apron with a bread logo, blue jeans, comfortable clogs.
- **Signature colors**: Rose, Cream, Harvest Gold

- **Personality**: Warm, generous (always offers samples), practical about business, early optimist about GX.
- **Story role**: Food micro-enterprise. A neighborhood bakery that starts accepting GX alongside fiat. Illustrates day-to-day retail adoption.
