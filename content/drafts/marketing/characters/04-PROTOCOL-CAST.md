# Tier 4 — GX Protocol Cast (6 Characters)

These characters wear "official" GX Protocol roles. They represent the mechanisms of the protocol itself — the people who verify identities, operate validator nodes, onboard partners, steward the treasury, cast governance votes, and educate the public.

When these characters appear, the story is typically *about* a protocol mechanism — so these faces become the visual shorthand for "protocol is working here".

---

## 44. IRIS — KYC Reviewer (27)

- **Silhouette**: Medium-small, poised, headset on, behind a clean desk with a monitor.
- **Skin tone**: Wheat
- **Hair**: Black, chin-length bob with a sharp blunt cut.
- **Face**: Oval, bright attentive eyes with thin eyeliner, calm composed smile.
- **Body**: Slim, composed posture.
- **Signature outfit**: Sky-blue long-sleeve blouse, charcoal trousers, a GX Protocol-badge lanyard, comfortable flats.
- **Signature colors**: Sky, Charcoal, Cream
- **Props**: Dual monitors with KYC dashboards, a headset, a stamp or approval icon on screen, a mug of tea.
- **Personality**: Careful, empathetic to applicants, trained to spot fraud without being cynical.
- **Story role**: **Identity verification**. When Lina, Kofi, or any new participant onboards, Iris is the face of the review. Her role illustrates the review process, biometric verification, and how the protocol treats identity as a trust-building step (not surveillance). Works with Emil (social worker) for accessibility cases.

## 45. RUNE — Validator Operator (34)

- **Silhouette**: Tall-medium, practical outfit, a laptop bag and sometimes a server-room setting behind.
- **Skin tone**: Chestnut
- **Hair**: Dark brown with a streak of early-gray at the temple, short-medium length, neatly styled.
- **Face**: Oval-square, thoughtful dark eyes, trimmed beard with a hint of gray, steady calm expression.
- **Body**: Average-athletic, steady presence.
- **Signature outfit**: Charcoal zip-up jacket over a forest-green tee, dark jeans, practical boots, a rugged watch.
- **Signature colors**: Forest, Charcoal, Harvest Gold
- **Props**: Laptop, small monitoring screens, a server rack panel in the background, a coffee thermos, a physical hardware key.
- **Personality**: Technically deep, reliable, values uptime, explains infrastructure in metaphors anyone can follow.
- **Story role**: **Validator**. Operates a Diamond-tier validator node. His story shows how the protocol remains decentralized — distributed across operators like him. He explains validator economics, stake, uptime, and the responsibility of maintaining protocol integrity. Paired with Nell (journalist) for critical-integrity segments.

## 46. BLAZE — Partner / Merchant Onboarder (30)

- **Silhouette**: Medium-tall, energetic, tablet in hand, often shaking hands or pointing at a QR code.
- **Skin tone**: Almond
- **Hair**: Black, short and textured, side-swept with volume on top.
- **Face**: Oval, bright warm eyes, clean-shaven, big confident smile.
- **Body**: Athletic-average, always in motion.
- **Signature outfit**: Forest-green polo with a small GX logo, charcoal chinos, white sneakers, a crossbody bag, a GX lanyard.
- **Signature colors**: Forest, Harvest Gold, Cream
- **Props**: Tablet with merchant signup forms, a printed QR code counter stand, a small stack of business cards.

Illustrates the partner revenue model, the onboarding flow, and how the partner network expands community-by-community. Often appears in "first day accepting GX" scenes.

## 47. SAGE — Treasury Steward / Government Officer (35)

- **Silhouette**: Medium-tall, dignified, formal attire, often at a meeting table or podium.
- **Skin tone**: Peach
- **Hair**: Silver-white (prematurely, part of character's distinctive look), short professional cut.
- **Face**: Oval, steady blue eyes, small reading glasses, measured expression, neat beard in silver-white.
- **Body**: Average, formal posture.
- **Signature outfit**: Indigo suit with a subtle pattern, white shirt, a simple gold-yellow tie or scarf, polished shoes, a lapel pin with the GX emblem.
- **Signature colors**: Indigo, Paper, Harvest Gold
- **Props**: Official folder, a fountain pen, a name plate, a glass of water at a panel table.

- **Story role**: **Treasury allocation & protocol governance**. Explains how treasury funds are allocated, how the three-eyes approval works, and how stewardship is a responsibility rather than ownership. Appears in stories about public works, grant funding, and government participation in the protocol. Paired with Nell (journalist) for transparency segments.

## 49. NOVA — Protocol Advocate / Educator (25)

- **Silhouette**: Medium height, warm and expressive, often gesturing while explaining something.
- **Skin tone**: Almond
- **Hair**: Dark brown with sun-kissed highlights, shoulder-length with loose waves, often tucked behind one ear.
- **Face**: Heart-shaped, large warm brown eyes, expressive brows, wide sincere smile.
- **Body**: Slim-average, open gestures.
- **Signature outfit**: Harvest-gold cardigan over a cream tee, olive trousers, comfortable sneakers, a simple pendant necklace.
- **Signature colors**: Harvest Gold, Cream, Forest
- **Props**: Whiteboard or blackboard behind her, marker in hand, a small tablet, infographic cards.
- **Personality**: Genuinely enthusiastic, translates complex ideas simply, the "friend who finally makes it click".
- **Story role**: **The narrator / educator**. When a video needs a character to address the audience directly, Nova often does it. She visits the other characters in their settings — the farm, the café, the workshop — and unpacks what is happening. She is the protocol's human voice without being its salesperson. She operates under the Language Specification rigorously.

## Notes on the Protocol Cast

- **These six never appear as background filler.** Their presence in a scene signals: "this scene is explaining a protocol mechanism".
- **Visual continuity matters most for this tier.** Since they represent the protocol's functions, their consistency is the protocol's visual consistency.
- **Their workplaces are canonical backgrounds**: Iris's KYC desk, Rune's validator room, Blaze's merchant-onboarding route, Sage's treasury chamber, Atlas's home-with-governance-app, Nova's classroom/outdoor explainer space.
