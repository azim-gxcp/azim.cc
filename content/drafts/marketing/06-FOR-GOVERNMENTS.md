## The Core Message

GX Protocol provides everything a CBDC promises — automatic treasury revenue, financial inclusion, cross-border settlement, and compliance infrastructure — without the development cost. The government's treasury receives an automatic allocation proportional to population. Participants receive grants they can spend immediately. Businesses receive interest-free capital. And the government paid nothing to build it.

## What Governments Receive

### Automatic Treasury Allocation

Government treasuries receive GX allocations proportional to their population's registration:
- GX 80 per participant for the first 2 billion globally
- GX 40 per participant for the next 2 billion

**Example**: A country of 200 million participants registering in the first phase receives approximately **GX 16 billion** (equivalent to approximately **USD 1.92 trillion** at the genesis reference rate).

### Ongoing Velocity Mechanism Revenue

40% of all velocity mechanism collections from participants in a government's jurisdiction flows automatically to that government's treasury — with:
- Zero collection infrastructure
- Zero auditing burden
- Zero evasion (enforced at protocol level)

Compare this to current fiscal reality: tax evasion costs governments approximately USD 427 billion annually (Tax Justice Network, 2024). Developing-country tax-to-GDP ratios often fall below 15%. The velocity mechanism is automatic, transparent, and impossible to evade.

### Financial Inclusion Infrastructure

Every GX participant is KYC-verified with biometric confirmation. This is a financial inclusion and identity infrastructure that many governments have spent decades trying to build. GX provides it at zero cost.

### Interest-Free Capital for Businesses

Registered businesses in the jurisdiction receive interest-free capital from the loan pool. This creates small business activity that generates velocity mechanism revenue independently of income taxation.

## Key Positioning Notes

### GX is Complementary, Not a Replacement
GX is presented exclusively as **complementary to national currency** — never as a substitute. Do not raise the topic of legal tender. GX adoption is 100% voluntary. A practical framing: participants continue to use national currency for domestic obligations while GX serves as the preferred medium for cross-border international transactions.

### Sovereignty is Preserved
GX does not ask governments to relinquish monetary policy. The national currency continues to function for all domestic purposes. GX supplements it — particularly for cross-border settlement, financial inclusion of unbanked populations, and automatic fiscal revenue.

### The Political Cost of Prohibition Grows with Adoption
A government that prohibits GX before it has 100,000 domestic participants faces minimal political opposition. A government that attempts to prohibit GX after 50 million domestic participants hold meaningful balances faces a constituency with a direct economic interest in resisting the prohibition.

## What NOT to Say to Governments

- Do NOT suggest replacing national currency with GX
- Do NOT claim GX will enable governments to restructure national debt in foreign currencies
- Do NOT present treasury allocation in USD equivalents only — present in GX first, then provide genesis reference rate as context
- Do NOT use the word "revolutionary" or suggest GX "disrupts" anything — regulators protect the existing system
- Do NOT promise specific regulatory outcomes
