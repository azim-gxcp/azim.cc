## The Core Message

You receive GX units at no cost. There is no financial risk — no purchase, no investment, no money at stake. Every participant has a role in making GX successful: by using the units productively, by transacting within the ecosystem, and by upholding the purchasing power of what was granted to them.

---

## What You Get

1. **GX units — for free.** Upon identity verification, you receive a grant of GX units. No purchase. No referral. No subscription.
2. **Send money anywhere for almost nothing.** A USD 200 transfer to another country costs USD 0.30-0.50 instead of USD 14-20.
3. **Your money doesn't lose value to inflation.** GX has a fixed supply — no government can print more.
4. **If you lose your phone, you don't lose your money.** Your identity is your recovery key.
5. **When you pass away, your balance goes to your verified heirs.** Automatically, through the protocol.
6. **Your identity is private.** Known to the protocol. Never disclosed to governments, companies, or third parties.
7. **Your data is never sold.** GX does not gather behavioural data, profile your activity, or monetise your information. You are not the product.

---

## What It Costs You

- **Money**: Nothing. Zero financial risk.
- **Time**: Identity verification takes [time to be confirmed at launch]
- **Personal data**: KYC verification requires identity documents and biometric confirmation. This data is encrypted and never shared with third parties.

---

## How To Participate

1. Complete identity verification (KYC) — one-time process
2. Receive your GX grant
3. Use GX to buy goods and services, send money, save
4. The more participants use GX productively, the stronger the ecosystem becomes

---

## What About the Velocity Mechanism?

If you earn GX and spend it regularly — buying goods, sending money, paying for services — you pay zero velocity tax. The velocity mechanism only applies to:

- Balances **above GX 100** (approximately USD 12,000 at genesis rate)
- Held for **more than 360 accumulated days** without use
- After **all your expenses are met** — it only touches idle surplus

If you're actively using GX as money, it costs you nothing beyond the transaction fee (0.05-0.40%).

## Stories (for video/article content)

### The Migrant Worker
A nurse in Dubai sends USD 200 home to the Philippines every month. Currently pays USD 14-20 in fees per transfer — that's USD 168-240/year lost to intermediaries. With GX: approximately USD 0.50 per transfer, saving USD 156-228/year. That's a month's rent in many Philippine provinces.

### The Small Business Owner
A tailor in Dhaka has been denied bank loans three times. Under GX, registered businesses can access interest-free capital through the loan pool. No interest. Repayment only when profitable. If the business succeeds, a share of profit goes back to the pool. If it fails after genuine effort, 50% of the loan is forgiven after 10 years.

### The Saver
A teacher in Nairobi saves monthly. In Kenyan Shillings, inflation eats her savings — the KES has lost significant purchasing power over the past 5 years. In GX, the supply is fixed. No one can print more. Her savings in GX maintain their protocol-defined value year after year.

## What NOT to Promise

When creating participant-facing content:
- Do NOT promise specific financial outcomes ("GX will be worth X")
- Do NOT say "nothing to lose" — say "no financial risk"
- Do NOT say "earning GX" — participants receive grants, not earnings
- Do NOT discuss failure scenarios
- Do NOT use the word "free money" — use "allocation grant"
- Do NOT compare to investments
